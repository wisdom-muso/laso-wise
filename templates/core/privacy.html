Privacy Policy

At DocCure, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
  • Name and contact information
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Medical history and conditions
  • Appointment records
  • Payment information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate appointment bookings
  • Improve our services
  • Communicate with you about appointments and services
  • Process payments
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your care
  • Payment processors for billing purposes
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@doccure.com
